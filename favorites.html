Shows

New Girl
This show first aired in 2011 and ended in 2018. The shows follows Jessica Day, a quirky school teacher that moves into a loft in Los Angeles with 3 men. Everyone is in their early 30's trying to navigate love and life. It is a mix of comedy and drama.
The Office
This shows aired from 2005 to 2013 and had total of nine seasons. It is a mockumentery and follows the daily work lives of employees at Dunder Mifflin Paper Company. The show is a comedy and is set in Scranton, Pennsylvania.
Grey's Anatomy
This show first aired in 2005 and is still in production today. It is currently on its 18th season. It is about the daily lives of medical interns, residents, and attendings at Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital. The series centers on Dr. Meredith Grey and her journey to become a doctor.
Downton Abbey
This show is a British historical drama that aired from 2010 to 2015. The show takes place between 1912 and 1926. It follows the lives of the wealthy Crawley family and their servants. Although the family and estate is fictional, the show does include certain historical events.
Gilmore Girls
This show aired from 2000 to 2007 with a revival special in 2016. The show focuses on the lives of 30 year old single mom Lorelai Gilmore and her intelligent teenage daughter Rory Gilmore. Lorelai's wealthy parents also become a subject of the show as Lorelai became pregnant at a young age and moved away from home. It is set in fictional Stars Hollow in Connecticut.
