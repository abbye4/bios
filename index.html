I was born on February 8, 1995 to Pedro and Bertha Magdaleno who resided in Brownsville, Texas. We lived as a family of 3 for 2 years until the birth of my younger brother. At this time, my parents decided that we should move our family to the DFW area. We would spend the next 8 years living in the city of Greenville, Texas. Although I do not consider it my hometown, I did spend a large part of my childhood and have many memories of this time.

I currently reside in Los Fresnos, Texas with my husband, Samuel Martinez. I am an 8th grade math teacher and teach at Resaca Middle School, where I have taught for 5 years. I love to read and drink lots of coffee. I enjoy to craft with my cricut. My family is very important to me as well as my faith. I am excited to see what the next 5 years bring.
